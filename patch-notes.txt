## Starting Five Income Rebalance
S5 income has been reduced across the board. Both flat income rates and reverse multiplier bonuses are scaled down to reach the intended target income.

Mythic and Unique S5 rates were **buffed** to compensate — these tiers now generate more relative value than before.

Bench effectiveness, allstar modifier, attachment bonuses, and team synergy are unchanged.

## Pack Pull Rate Buff
Odds to pull **Epic, Legendary, Mythic, and Unique** cards have been increased across all packs.

## Consumable Rework
The permanent passive consumable slot has been replaced with a **3-slot use-and-destroy system**. Consumables are now single-use items that are consumed on activation.

**7 Consumable Types:**
- **Health Potion** — Fill X% of Cores cap instantly
- **Mana Potion** — +X% income rate until collect
- **Multi Potion** — +X% rate AND +X days cap bonus until collect
- **Elixir of Strength** — Multiply next claim payout by X
- **Elixir of Intelligence** — Expand dismantle thresholds
- **Vision Ward** — Add X days to cap until collect
- **Sentry Ward** — Random 1–X Cores (instant)

All consumables scale by rarity and give instant effects or temporary buffs (last until next claim). At Mythic: Mana Potion gives +300% rate, Elixir of Strength gives 2.30x payout, Sentry Ward rolls 1–500 Cores.

## Rarity Guarantee Fix
Special packs with rarity guarantees could silently fail due to a format mismatch. A normalization step now handles both formats correctly, so all pack guarantees are properly enforced.
