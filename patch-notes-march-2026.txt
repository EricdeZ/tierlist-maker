# SmiteComp Update — March 2026

## Patch Notes Viewer
Browse SMITE 2 balance updates right on the site. Each patch has a detailed breakdown of every god and item change — stat diffs are color-coded (green = buff, red = nerf), and there's a TL;DR mode if you just want the highlights. Find it in the sidebar or head to https://smitecomp.com/patchnotes

## Tradeable Packs
Packs can now be traded and listed on the marketplace.
- List packs for sale on the marketplace (2% fee, same as cards)
- Separate listing cap of 5 active pack listings per user

## Rotating Special Packs
Completing rotating challenges now awards **Challenge Packs** — a special pack type exclusive to this system. Your challenge pool rotates on a schedule, so the packs you can earn change with each period. The harder the challenge, the more packs you get.

## Unique Cards
A new **Unique Cards** section has been added to the Vault under the More dropdown.
- View all your unique-rarity cards with full-size renders
- **Holo Switcher** — freely swap between Holo, Reverse, and Full Art effects
- **Signatures** — request a signature on player cards and track their status
- https://smitecomp.com/vault?tab=unique

## Daily Challenges
The Vault now has **daily, weekly, and monthly rotating challenges**. Each period you get a random set of objectives — open packs, complete trades, dismantle cards, and more.
- **Daily:** 3 challenges, resets at midnight UTC
- **Weekly:** 4 challenges, resets Monday
- **Monthly:** 3 challenges, resets 1st of the month
- Rewards include Cores, Challenge Packs, or both
- Countdown timers show when challenges reset
- 1-hour grace period to claim after a period ends
- https://smitecomp.com/vault?tab=challenges

## Drop Rate Tuning
Rare pulls have been buffed across the board. Epic, Legendary, Mythic, and Unique cards are all more likely to appear in packs.

## NEW Badge
When opening packs, cards you're seeing for the first time now show a **NEW** badge on the flip reveal.

## Collection & Marketplace Filters
The collection and marketplace have been updated with better filtering and sorting options.
- **Collection:** filter by rarity, sort by name/rarity/duplicates, toggle owned vs all, and a new Recent Pulls section showing your last 8 cards
- **Marketplace:** filter by item type (cards/packs), rarity, card type, holo type, and role. Sort by price, rarity, or date. New "Show Stats" toggle to preview card effects before buying
- https://smitecomp.com/vault?tab=marketplace
