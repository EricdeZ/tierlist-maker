# **Patch Notes — March 15, 2026**

## __The Vault__

**Unique Rarity**
- New rarity tier above Mythic for player cards
- Custom holographic effect with layered radiant + rainbow shine
- Available across collection, marketplace, trading, Starting Five, bounties, and packs

**Off-Role God Variants**
- 47 new god card variants (G-078 to G-124) for gods played in non-standard roles
- Now pullable from packs

**Marketplace & Collection**
- New search and filter options on the marketplace
- Recent pulls section in collection
- Binder sharing — show off your collection to others
- Subtabs in Marketplace, Trading, and Gifts are now shareable via URL

**Leaderboards**
- New Starting Five leaderboard — top 20 highest-rated lineups
- New Top Gifters leaderboard in the Gifts tab

**Dismantle**
- Added holo type filter
- Cards listed on marketplace or locked in trades no longer appear in dismantle

**Bounty Board**
- Bounties can now target specific off-role god variants and specific player card versions
- Fixed avatar display on older bounties

**Player Card Avatars**
- Fixed stale avatars across marketplace, trading, collection, and Starting Five — avatars now always show the latest Discord profile picture
