Spectral Visage (Removed)
Spectral Armor's rework has focused it on being a more all-in-one counter to Critical Strikes. Spectral Visage was always a band-aid solution for Magical Critical Strikes; but the combination of Plating and mitigating ability Critical hits should cover all cases.
