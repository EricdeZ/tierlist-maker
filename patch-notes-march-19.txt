## ⭐ Starting 5 — Reward Redesign

Starting 5 now has **two lineups**: **Current Season** and **All-Star**.

**Current Season** is your main lineup — slot players from the active season and earn at the full rate. **All-Star** lets you slot cards from any season, past or present, but earns at a reduced rate. Cards are exclusive to one lineup — you can't double-dip.

Each lineup also has a **Bench slot** — a 6th slot with no role restriction that earns at half effectiveness. Good for parking a card you want to keep ready.

### How earning works now

Every card contributes two things: a **flat income** (Passion + Cores per day) and a **multiplier** that scales your entire lineup's output. Higher rarity means more of both.

> 🟡 **Holo** cards provide the flat income — your baseline earning
> 🔵 **Reverse holo** cards provide the multiplier — they amplify everything else
> 🟣 **Full art** cards give both

Attachments work the same way — a holo god/item attachment boosts your flat values, a reverse attachment adds to the multiplier. God attachments are stronger than item attachments. God synergy and team synergy bonuses still apply.

**Consumable slot** — Slot a consumable card (health pots, wards, elixirs, etc.) for a rarity-scaled boost. Different consumables lean toward Passion or Cores, so you can tune your earnings.

The lineup summary now shows a **full breakdown** of your flat income, combined multiplier, and total rate per hour — no more guessing.

## ✒️ Card Signatures

Players can now **sign Unique cards** that depict them.

Pull a Unique card of a player? You can **request their autograph**. They get a notification in their Vault and can draw their signature directly on a canvas overlaid on the card — with a **live preview** showing exactly how it'll look with holo effects.

It's a **two-step process**:
1. The depicted player draws and submits their signature
2. The card owner reviews it and approves or rejects — rejecting lets the player redo it

Players who own their **own** Unique card can sign it directly — no request needed.

Signed cards display the signature **permanently** on the card art, visible everywhere: collection, binder, marketplace, and Starting 5.

## 🔄 Role Detection — Most Played, Not Last Played

Role detection now uses **total games played per role** instead of the single most recent game. If a player has 15 games as Support and 1 recent game as Mid, they'll correctly show as a Support main.
