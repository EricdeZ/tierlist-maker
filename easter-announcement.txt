# 🐣 smitecomp.com Easter Invitational

**Sign-ups are officially OPEN!**
**https://smitecomp.com/tournaments/easter-invitational**

Our very first tournament is here — an auction draft where captains bid to build their dream rosters. All skill levels welcome, cross-platform play enabled.

**8 teams. 3 days. One champion.**

## 📅 Dates
- **April 7** — Live Auction Draft
- **April 8** — Quarterfinals (Bo1) & Semifinals (Bo3)
- **April 9** — Grand Finals (Bo5)

All days kick off at **8:00 PM EST** (check-in at 7:30)

## 💎 Prizes
🥇 1st — **5,000 Gems**
🥈 2nd — **2,500 Gems**
🥉 3rd — **1,000 Gems**

Everyone also gets exclusive tournament player cards, card packs, and Passion rewards just for playing!

## How it works
Sign up, and if you're selected you'll get an invite a week before the draft. Captains get a budget to bid on players in a live auction — go all-in on your star player or spread it out for a balanced squad.

Think you've got what it takes to lead a team? Apply as captain during signup.
